Dua Lipa

ABOUT

Dua Lipa is a British popstar who sings and writes her own songs. She first started her music career in 2014 when she signed with Warner Records, an American record label owned by Warner Bros. She released her first single, "New Love," in August 2015. She debuted her first album, Dua Lipa, in June 2017. This album included the song "New Rules" which is her best-selling single ever released. It topped the UK's charts and was within the top ten of many other countries' charts including Australia (#2), the United States (#6), and Canada (#7) shortly after its release. Her second album, Future Nostalgia, was released in March 2020. The album peaked at the #1 spot on the Official UK Albums chart shortly after its release. Four of the album's songs were released as singles, two of which, "Don't Start Now" and "Levitating", reached the top ten of the US Billboard Hot 100. Future Nostalgia was the most streamed album by a woman and the fifth most streamed overall on Spotify in 2020. Dua Lipa released her third and most recent album, Radical Optimism, in May 2024. Throughout her career, she has released many additional singles not featured on albums including collaborations with other artists and song contributions to films.

Dua Lipa is currently on her Radical Optimism Tour, performing live concerts in many locations around the world. It began in November 2024 and will conclude on December 5, 2025 with her final show in Mexico City, Mexico. You can view the remaining tour dates and purchase tickets here.
